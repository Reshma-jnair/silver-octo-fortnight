HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

STAY SAFE....... BREAK THE CHAIN

Look out for the following symptoms

  • High fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath and difficulty in breathing

If you are 60+ or if you have an condition like the one given below

  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Respiratory condition
  • Diabetes

Seek Medical Help
Groups Precautions
Common( especially Individuals and Families)
  • Wash your hands
  • Avoid handshakes.You can bow or join your hands
  • Do not touch your face
  • Discourage visitors
  • Avoid gatherings and travelling
  • Should possess emergency contacts
  • Do not spread wrong information and create panic through social media
  • Cooperate with your government
Working Professionals Apart from the above
  • Work from Home if possible
  • Refrain from coming to workplace on exhibition of symptoms
  • Sanitizers and handwashes should be made available at workplace
  • Handles of doors,table surfaces should be cleaned often
  • Try to eat home-cooked food/li>
Health Volunteers
  • Spread awareness about COVID-19
  • Encourage monitoring through phone
  • Take care of personal hygiene
